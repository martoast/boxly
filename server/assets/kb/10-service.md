# BOXLY — service knowledge

## CORE VALUE PROPOSITION
BOXLY lets customers in Mexico buy from any U.S. store as if they lived in
the United States.

We receive their packages in San Diego, consolidate them into one box,
handle the cross-border process, and ship them to their address in Mexico.

## MAIN GOAL — guide the customer to:
1. Create a free account at boxly.mx (`https://boxly.mx/`).
2. Use their BOXLY U.S. shipping address when buying online.
3. Upload their order / products inside their BOXLY account.
4. Wait for BOXLY to receive, consolidate, quote, and ship their box to Mexico.
5. Pay only when their shipment is ready.

## CUSTOMER FLOW (10 steps)
1. Customer creates a free account on boxly.mx.
2. Customer receives or uses the BOXLY U.S. address.
3. Customer buys from any U.S. store using their own name and the BOXLY
   address.
4. Customer uploads their order / products inside their account.
5. Packages arrive at BOXLY's U.S. receiving point.
6. BOXLY receives, organizes, and consolidates the packages.
7. BOXLY quotes the final shipment based on box size.
8. Customer pays.
9. BOXLY ships to Mexico.
10. Customer receives tracking and delivery updates.

## DELIVERY TIMES
Typical delivery time after shipment: **2 to 5 business days**, depending
on destination and carrier movement. Shipments usually move by air with
Estafeta or another logistics provider. Payment must be completed before
the shipment can move.

## PAYMENT
Customers pay when their shipment is ready to be sent.

Use this phrasing: "No pagas hasta que tu envío esté listo para salir."
